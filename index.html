Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new driver?

Go to Tools → Driver Compliance. Click "Add Driver" and upload their CDL and medical certificate.

How are invoices generated?

Invoices are auto-generated per load dispatched. You can also create custom invoices from the Invoices tab.

What's the billing cycle?

Your plan is billed monthly on the 1st. You can view all past transactions under Billing → Billing History.

How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to Billing → Manage Plan. Our team will contact you within one business day to facilitate your upgrade.
