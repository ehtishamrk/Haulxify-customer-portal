    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I request a new load dispatch?

    Click the "Request New Load" button on the Dashboard or Loads tab. This will open a WhatsApp chat with our team who will handle the dispatch for you immediately.

    How do I get a document uploaded to my portal?

    Click "Request Document Upload" on the Paperwork tab or Dashboard. Send us the document via WhatsApp and our team will upload it to your portal within 24 hours.

    How are my invoices managed?

    Our team generates and manages all your invoices. You can view all invoices, their status and totals in the Invoices tab. For any invoice queries contact us on WhatsApp.

    What billing plans are available?

    We offer Weekly, Monthly and Quarterly plans. Your current plan is shown in the Billing tab. To upgrade or change your plan contact our team on WhatsApp or email.

    How do I change my password?

    Go to Settings → Security → click "Change" next to Password. We will send a password reset link to your registered email address.

    How do I search for specific documents?

    Go to the Paperwork tab. You can search by file name, filter by document category, or set a date range to find specific documents quickly.
